## English

This data is provided for informational purposes only and does not constitute legally binding guidance on direct payment entitlements.

The authoritative sources are:

- The **Swiss Direct Payments Ordinance (DZV, SR 910.13)**: [fedlex.admin.ch](https://www.fedlex.admin.ch/eli/cc/2013/765/de)
- **BLW directives and explanations**: [blw.admin.ch](https://www.blw.admin.ch/blw/de/home/instrumente/direktzahlungen.html)
- **Cantonal implementing regulations** of the relevant canton
- The **Agate portal** for farm-specific calculations: [agate.ch](https://www.agate.ch/)

Changes introduced through agricultural policy revisions (AP 22+) or parliamentary initiatives must be verified independently. For binding information, contact the cantonal agricultural office (kantonales Landwirtschaftsamt) or the direct payments office of your canton.
